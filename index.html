About us

Brewed with love in every cup

Happy Cup was born from a simple idea: coffee should make you feel good. Not just from the caffeine buzz, but from knowing that your brew is ethical, fresh, and full of flavour. We source our beans from smallholder farms around the world, roast them right here in-house, and serve every cup with a smile.

We're more than just a coffee shop—we're a community of coffee lovers who care about people, the planet, and, of course, great taste. Stop by our shop or bring the joy home. Either way, you're one sip away from a happier day.
